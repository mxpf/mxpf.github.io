For the past three weeks or so I've been experimenting with eating a future food called Soylent. No, it doesn't have people in it. I don't think.

Soylent is a powdered meal in a bag that you mix with water a little vat they send you. Shake it up and you have a little scifi tank filled with precisely 2000 calories of a vegetarian food substance that satisfies all of your daily nutritional requirements. It tastes like IKEA smells: cozy, with a hint of...cinnamon? and that subtle hint of plywood offgassing.
